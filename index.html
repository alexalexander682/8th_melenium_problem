About the 8th Millennium Problem

The 8th Millennium Problem represents humanity’s next grand scientific, philosophical, and technological challenge — an intellectual summit that aims to shape the future of civilization.

Building on the legacy of the seven Millennium Prize Problems, the 8MP seeks to address a new frontier: one that could redefine life, intelligence, and existence itself.

Our Mission

To push human thought beyond its current boundaries and inspire future generations to achieve breakthroughs that echo across history.
